What do we mean by “semantic”?

Semantic elements describe their purpose (better accessibility, SEO, and structure).

Semantic elements

<header> <nav> <main> <section> <article> <aside> <footer> <figure> <figcaption>

Non-semantic elements

<div> (block), <span> (inline) — generic containers with no meaning.
